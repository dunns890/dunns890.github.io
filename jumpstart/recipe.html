Lemon Blueberry Dutch Baby Recipe

Ingredients

  • flour
  • milk
  • eggs
  • butter
  • vanilla extract
  • lemon juice
  • blueberries

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 425F
  2. Mix all ingredients except for the butter
  3. Melt the butter on the stove top
  4. Place blueberries in cast iron skillet
  5. Pour in mixture
  6. Place in oven until puffed and golden brown for 20 minutes

Flavor Pairings

Fruit Spice Sweetener
Peach Nutmeg Brown Sugar
Apple Cinnamon Caramel
